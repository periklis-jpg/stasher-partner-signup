Best practices — how to use Stasher

01

Add your affiliate link everywhere

Include your referral link in your website, email newsletters, booking confirmation emails, and social media bio. The more touchpoints, the more bookings.

02

Target high-intent moments

The best time to promote Stasher is when travellers are planning their trip — in booking confirmations, check-in emails, or "things to do" guides. Offer storage as a convenience.

03

Highlight the customer benefit

Focus on what matters to your audience: secure, insured luggage storage from just £1/hour, available at thousands of locations worldwide. No queues, no hassle.

04

Use your coupon code

If you have a coupon code, promote it actively. Discounts drive conversions. Include it in every communication and make it clear it's exclusive to your audience.

05

Add Stasher to your confirmation emails

When guests confirm a booking or accommodation with you, add a short line: "Need somewhere to store your bags? Our partner Stasher has you covered — your link here."

06

Grow your network

Share your recruitment link with other businesses in hospitality, travel, or tourism. Every new active affiliate you bring in adds to your earning potential.
